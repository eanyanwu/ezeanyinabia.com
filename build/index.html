Who I am

My name is Eze and I am interested in computers and how they can be used. I currently specialize in Back End web development.

How I got here

I studied Computer Science and Mathematics at Gordon College in Wenham, MA.

During my third year in college, I rediscovered my passion for learning and directed it towards web development. I was then fortunate to be given the opportunity to work on my skills by developing web applications for my school. You can read more about the work I did from my resume and LinkedIn profile.

After I graduated, I was given the opportunity to be part of the first cohort for Hack Diversity; an organization committed to bringing more diversity into the innovation space. My experience with them gave me the chance to enter the tech scene in person.

Where I am going

I want to be part of a close community of motivated individuals who share my passion for technology.

I want to be challenged to work in areas where I am not yet comfortable like network engineering, hardware and operating system internals. I also want to be continually challenged to improve in the areas I think I am good at.

I want to be part of a team that is working hard on creating technology that empowers its users and makes them significantly better off.
